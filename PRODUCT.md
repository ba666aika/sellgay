# PRODUCT.md — loyalty

Loaded by Impeccable on every `/impeccable *` command. Keep this stable —
when the user updates lore/identity, edit this file (not the code).

## Audience

Pump.fun memecoin holders who feel rugged by short-term whales. The audience
isn't crypto-curious normies — it's people who already lost to bots and pre-mints,
and who recognize "loyalty" as a counter-mechanism rather than a slogan.

They are reading fast, often on mobile, often in a Telegram link preview.
They have ~3 seconds to decide if the mechanic is real.

## Promise

The longer you hold, the bigger your share of the rewards. Buy size is irrelevant.
Any sell resets you to zero. That's the whole product.

## Voice

Plain. Direct. No hype. No exclamation points. No "🚀". Numbers over adjectives.
"Held 12h 4m" not "Diamond hands certified!". Treat the reader like an adult.

## Anti-references (Impeccable will reject these)

- Purple gradient backgrounds.
- "Boost your", "Unlock", "Supercharge" copy.
- Cards-on-cards-on-cards layouts.
- Glassmorphism.
- Cartoon mascots.
- Roadmaps with fake milestones.
- "Coming soon" anywhere.

## Commands the bot/site must support

- View own held time and current share.
- View global bubble map (every wallet sized by held time).
- Connect wallet read-only (no transaction signing, ever).
